About Us

At ModelToyCars.com, we don't just sell diecast cars. Our mission is to provide you a friendly, informative, trustworthy and worry-free diecast vehicle buying experience. We want you to feel the old-style shopping experience of the local hobby shop while enjoying the convenience of the web. In short, we want to be The World's Most Reliable Source of Diecast Model Toy Cars. In reality, we're just a couple of guys with several decades of experience dealing with diecast model toy cars and a few years' experience selling stuff on the web. After reading lots of complaints about the current leading online diecast sellers -- and experiencing some problems firsthand -- we've decided to change the way diecast vehicles are sold online.
